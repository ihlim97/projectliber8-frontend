About

Project Liber8

Project Liber8 is a non-profit organization dedicated to shifting attitudes around human trafficking. We do this by engaging the public to action through youth mobilisation, public education, technology, research and creating strong partnerships.

Su Shern founded the organization at the age of 19 years old when she came across a documentary on human trafficking which compelled her to start advocating for the cause. Despite what others may think of "millenials", she believes that we do care about social issues and there is something powerful we can all contribute to. WIth the idea of tackling what may seem like the most complicated global issue targeting to an audience of youths that are known to be "hard to please" - Project Liber8 is set out to create an epic journey with you.
