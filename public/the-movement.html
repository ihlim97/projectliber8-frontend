Volunteer Program

Register yourself as a volunteer!

Do you want to make a change but don’t know where to start? Or want to donate your skills and time for good? We hear you say YES and this is a perfect opportunity for us to connect! Project Liber8 is dynamic and always on the lookout for passionate people and great ideas.

Here’s what you can expect as a volunteer:

  • We will match your unique skill set and availability with our partner NGOs, so that you can be awesome at assisting them on specific tasks
  • Help us run cool events, or organise your own events with our support
  • Receive insider updates on meetups and exclusive events before anyone else
  • ]We value your opinion: share your ideas on how you think Project Liber8 can do better!

Lobby With Us

Take the next step to combat human trafficking

You'll play an important part in helping us lobby for better laws against human trafficking. Here's how you can get involved.

  • Attend an initial meetup to learn more about the issue and what we are lobbying for
  • Get a training session (1 day) on how to approach MPs in Parliament
  • Participate on lobbying day and put your training into action. We’ll do this together, so don’t worry about feeling alone!

The Story Behind Project Liber8

Overview
This is always a crowd pleaser! In this session, we share about our journey at Project Liber8 - how we started the organization, the challenges we faced, breaking the status-quo and of course, why we do what we do. Along the way, we’ve learned about grit, perseverance and getting outside of our comfort zone to pursue an idea we believe so strongly about. This session is about inspiring the next generation of changemakers towards a #SlaveFreeMalaysia.

Audience
Form 1-5 and College or University Students

Length
40 minutes to 70 minutes

Focus
Human trafficking | Non profit organization | Starting from an idea

Outcomes

  • Gain greater awareness about human trafficking
  • Learn about running a nonprofit organization
  • Recognizing how they can create an impact with their talent and skill
  • Learn about the importance of determination, grit and perseverance
