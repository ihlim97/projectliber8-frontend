Advoc8 Series

Inspiring the next wave of global changemakers

Advoc8 On The Road explains what human trafficking is and why we started the initiative to fight against the issue. Our sessions talk about the realities of the issue and how we aspire to create bold campaigns to empower others to make a change. This programme challenges youths as they take their next step in creating anti-trafficking campaigns that will broaden their horizons, daring them to dream big and taking bold actions. In these sessions, the participants will not only get clear and comprehensive knowledge on human trafficking but also an exclusive peek at Project Liber8’s approach to innovative campaigns and solutions-focused thinking.

The Story Behind Project Liber8

Overview
This is always a crowd pleaser! In this session, we share about our journey at Project Liber8 - how we started the organization, the challenges we faced, breaking the status-quo and of course, why we do what we do. Along the way, we’ve learned about grit, perseverance and getting outside of our comfort zone to pursue an idea we believe so strongly about. This session is about inspiring the next generation of changemakers towards a #SlaveFreeMalaysia.

Audience
Form 1-5 and College or University Students

Length
40 minutes to 70 minutes

Focus
Human trafficking | Non profit organization | Starting from an idea

Outcomes

  • Gain greater awareness about human trafficking
  • Learn about running a nonprofit organization
  • Recognizing how they can create an impact with their talent and skill
  • Learn about the importance of determination, grit and perseverance
