Project Liber8 aims to use technology as part of our core initiative to counter the issue.

Big Data Analysis

With the rise of sophisticated technological platforms, from social media platforms, accessibility of online advertisements, encrypted messaging apps and other communication platforms, human traffickers now have more opportunities than ever to reach out to more potential victims with technology.

While new technologies become increasingly advanced, it is also the main key in helping us eradicate the problem. Some examples of great tech related initiative are used to discover and rescue victims, sharing and analyzing data from hotlines, locating trafficking activity or using big data analysis to create prevention campaigns.

If you have any inquiries or want to contribute in this initiative, you can email us to get in touch!
